Procyon Skies

Instructions

Welcome! Enter your name, and get ready for blastoff!

Arrow keys to move. z for blasters. x for torpedos. c to orbit to a planet.

You can also use WASD to move, "," for blasters, and "." for torpedos.

Score points by blasting asteroids, blasting other players, and collecting resources.
